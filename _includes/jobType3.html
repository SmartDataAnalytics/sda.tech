Requirements

  • Proficiency in spoken and written English.

Interest in “one of” (not necessarily more) the following areas:

  • Big Data, Machine Learning, Data Mining
  • Semantic Technologies and Linked Data
  • Geo spatial data modeling and analysis
  • Natural language processing, in particular Question Answering

We offer:

  • You will be able to efficiently work towards your doctorate in a group of excellent scientists.
  • You will work at one of the leading [1] German Universities.
  • You will enjoy a close collaboration with Fraunhofer IAIS [2] as a leading research institute for large scale machine learning and data mining.
  • The payment will be according to the standard payment scale for student assistants.

To apply, please send a mail to SDA Applications including a CV, a master certificate, a motivation letter including a short overview of previous research activities, two letters of recommendation and a writing sample (e.g. prior publication or master thesis excerpt). Applications are possible until all positions have been filled. Please do not send mails larger than 10MB.


The University of Bonn is an equal opportunities employer.
